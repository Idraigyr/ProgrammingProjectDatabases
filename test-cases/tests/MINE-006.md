# Mine Menu Functionality Test Cases

## Test Case 6: Mine Menu Crystal Generation Test

- **Test Case ID:** MINE-006
- **Test Case Description:** Verify that the mine menu passively generates crystals for the player. Additionally, ensure that the player can collect the crystals by clicking on the collect button.
- **Preconditions:** 
  - The player has access to the mine menu.
  - The mine menu is accessible and functional.
- **Test Steps:** 
  1. Monitor Crystal Generation:
     - Open the mine menu and observe the passive generation of crystals.
     - Record the rate at which crystals are generated over a period of time.
  2. Collect Crystals:
     - Click on the collect button in the mine menu to collect the generated crystals.
     - Ensure that the number of collected crystals is added to the player's inventory.
- **Expected Results:** 
  - The mine menu should passively generate crystals for the player at a consistent rate.
  - Clicking on the collect button should add the generated crystals to the player's inventory.
- **Postconditions:** 
  - The player should have collected the generated crystals from the mine menu, which can be used for various in-game purposes.
- **Notes:** 
  - Verify that the crystal rates align with the game's design specifications.
  - Ensure that the collect button functions smoothly and accurately adds the collected crystals to the player's inventory.
  - Reload the game to confirm that the mine menu continues to generate crystals after a save/load cycle.
